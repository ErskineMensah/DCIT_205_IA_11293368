SHORT PROGRAMMES

The Mobile Application Development short course provides a comprehensive introduction to the principles and practices of creating mobile applications for various platforms such as iOS and Android. Students will learn about the fundamentals of mobile app development, including user interface design, programming languages, and app deployment. Through hands-on projects and practical exercises, participants will gain the skills and knowledge necessary to develop their own mobile applications. This course is ideal for individuals interested in pursuing a career in mobile app development or for those looking to enhance their technical skills.

The "Introduction to Web Programming " is a dynamic short program offered by the Computer Science Department. Participants will delve into the essentials of web design and development, gaining valuable insights into HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Whether you're a beginner or looking to enhance your skills, this program provides a solid foundation for creating interactive and responsive websites. From understanding the fundamentals to exploring web frameworks, participants will engage in hands-on projects, building a portfolio that showcases their newfound expertise. Don't miss this opportunity to acquire in-demand skills and kickstart your web development journey. Register now to secure your spot and unlock the door to a world of digital possibilities.

The CCNA short course introduces networking concepts and Cisco technologies. Students will gain foundational knowledge in areas such as network security, routing, and switching. This course is ideal for individuals looking to start a career in networking or seeking to enhance their understanding of Cisco networking technologies

The Information Security and Ethical Hacking short course offers an overview of cybersecurity principles and ethical hacking practices. Students will learn about threat analysis, vulnerability assessment, and risk management, gaining hands-on experience with ethical hacking techniques and tools. This course is suitable for those interested in cybersecurity careers or seeking to enhance their technical skills in this field.
