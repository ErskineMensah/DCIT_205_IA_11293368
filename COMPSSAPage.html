COMPSSA UoG

ABOUT

This is the official page for the “Computer Science Students Association of University of Ghana”. The abbreviation of the association name is “COMPSSA-UoG”. The Slogan is: “Genius!” with response: “A step ahead”. The Computer Science Students' Association of the University of Ghana (COMPSSA UoG) is a dynamic and vibrant community that serves as the epicenter for all things related to computer science within the university and is committed to fostering a collaborative and innovative environment.

MISSION AND VISION

This Association aims to promote and protect the positive reputation of the Computer Science Students and transform them into problem solvers after school. It also desires to create a social environment where students can thrive by interacting and sharing ideas with each other for personal growth and development. COMPSSA focuses on building a sense of camaraderie among its members, facilitating interactions between students of different academic levels. Through mentorship programs and peer-to-peer support, the association encourages collaboration and the sharing of ideas, fostering a community that thrives on collective learning.

EVENTS

The association is dedicated to enriching the academic experience of computer science students by organizing a diverse range of events, including workshops, seminars, and hackathons. These events aim to provide students with practical insights into the latest trends in technology, programming languages, and emerging fields within the realm of computer science.
