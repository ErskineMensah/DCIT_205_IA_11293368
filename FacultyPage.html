FACULTY

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science, where innovation meets education, and the future of technology begins. At the Department of Computer Science, we are proud to foster a dynamic and innovative learning environment that prepares students for the ever-evolving field of computer science. Our distinguished faculty members bring a wealth of expertise and experience, contributing to the department's reputation for academic excellence. Explore the profiles of our esteemed faculty members to learn more about their research, expertise, and contributions to the field. Whether you are a prospective student, a current student, or a visitor, we invite you to connect with our faculty and discover the exciting world of computer science at our department.
