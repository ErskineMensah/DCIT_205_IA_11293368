Our Facilities

Student Reading room And Library

The department believes that knowledge is the key to unlocking endless possibilities. Our Student Reading Room and Library are designed to be vibrant spaces that foster a love for learning, exploration, and academic excellence.

Hardware engineering Lab

We here at the computer science department understand the pivotal role that hands-on experience plays in the field of computer science.
